Keys like rain in steady flow,
currents hum in circuits below,
a lantern lit in midnight hours,
crafting castles from unseen powers.

A pane of glass both stage and pen,
a film, a game, a brush again,
it hums with play yet still imparts,
a vessel of light for crafted arts.

Small glowing glass held in each hand,
a tether to voices across the land,
whispers of worlds both near and far,
pocket-sized mirrors of who we are.
